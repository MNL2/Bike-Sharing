Bike sharing is bike rental system.

How docking stations work Bike share stations are easily one of the best real-time applications of automation in this era of increasing road traffic. These rental stations basically have a docking system with varying sizes (generally between 10 to 100) and a convenient no-human-interaction-required policy.

Here's the crazy part: What happens if a certain number of people go from place A to place B every day? Wouldn't that create a shortage at place A and an influx at place B? For that, company employees regularly move the bikes around the city.
